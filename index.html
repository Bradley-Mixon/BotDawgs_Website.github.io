Bowie Robotics

The Bowie BotDawgs Robotics Club is a great extra-curricular activity for any grade level of JBHS students who want to learn and apply engineering skills that are industry applicable in a fun and friendly environment. No experience is necessary, and students do not need to be enrolled in a computer science or engineering class to participate.
