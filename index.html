About Us

THE BOWIE BOTDAWGS IS THE FIRST ROBOTICS CLUB AT JBHS

Most people know of these words by inspirational leader Mahatma Gandhi "Be the change you want to see in the world." But, for the members of the Bowie Botdawg Robotics Club, these are far more than just words, they're a principle that can be put into action. The Bowie Botdawgs Robotics Club was founded in 2017 by the efforts of a small but dedicated group of students who recognized that many Bowie students possess the talent, desire and dedication towards improving the world through robotics and technology.
